**`totalObligatedAmountPa`** — total $ FEMA committed for Public Assistance (infrastructure, debris, emergency measures). This is what we've been using as the target.

**`totalObligatedAmountCatAb`** — subset of PA obligations specifically for Category A (debris removal) and B (emergency protective measures). Early response costs only.

**`totalObligatedAmountCatC2g`** — subset of PA obligations for Categories C through G (roads, buildings, utilities, permanent repairs). Long term recovery costs.

**`totalObligatedAmountHmgp`** — Hazard Mitigation Grant Programme obligations. Money spent on preventing future disasters, not recovering from this one.

**`totalAmountIhpApproved`** — Individual & Households Programme approvals. Money going directly to affected individuals and families.

**`totalAmountHaApproved`** — Housing Assistance subset of IHP. Rent, repairs, temporary housing.

**`totalAmountOnaApproved`** — Other Needs Assistance subset of IHP. Medical, childcare, personal property.

**`totalNumberIaApproved`** — count of approved Individual Assistance applications.
